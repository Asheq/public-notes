# In Normal mode
    `{count}>>`
        Indent `{count}` LINES once

# In Visual mode
    `{count}>`
        Indent selected lines `{count}` TIMES

# In Command-line mode
    `:> {count}`
        Indent `{count}` LINES once
    `:>>> {count}`
        Indent `{count}` LINES thrice

    `:[range]>`
        Indent lines in RANGE once
    `:[range]>>>`
        Indent lines in RANGE thrice
